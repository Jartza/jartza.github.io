Long time no blogging...

May 22, 2019
1 min read

Welcome to Firebay Electro Plumbing -blog.

It has been around 11 years since my last real blogging attempt and while I find Facebook to be nice platform for ad hoc -stories, I find the short lifetime of stories in feed and the search functionality quite lacking.

Hence I decided to put up a blog platform to document my hobby electronics projects, retro computer refurbishing stories and just general stuff I find interesting.

I will also copy some older stuff to this blog, that have been lost from the Internet already. Those old posts might retain datestamps that are actually in the past compared to this welcome -post, do not be alarmed, it’s not time machine, it’s Wayback Machine.
